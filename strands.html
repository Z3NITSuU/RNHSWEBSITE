HUMSS
"HUMSS" typically refers to the academic track in the Senior High School program in the Philippines. HUMSS stands for Humanities and Social Sciences. This track is designed to provide students with a strong foundation in the fields of humanities, social sciences, education, and liberal arts. The HUMSS track aims to develop critical thinking, communication skills, and a deep understanding of social issues.
STEM
"STEM" stands for Science, Technology, Engineering, and Mathematics. It is an academic discipline that integrates these four key areas of study. The STEM field is characterized by an interdisciplinary and applied approach, focusing on solving real-world problems and encouraging critical thinking, innovation, and creativity. The STEM education system aims to prepare students for careers in fields that require expertise in science, technology, engineering, and mathematics.
ICT
"ICT" stands for Information and Communication Technology. It is a broad term that encompasses technologies used to handle telecommunications, broadcast media, audio-visual processing and transmission systems, intelligent building ... management systems, network-based control and monitoring functions, and various applications of computers and computer networks. In an educational context, ICT may refer to a subject or track that focuses on teaching students about the effective use of technology for communication, information processing, and problem-solving. This can include both hardware and software components, as well as the use of digital tools and resources for various purposes.
HE
Home economics courses may be offered at various educational levels, from high schools to universities. The goal is to equip individuals with practical skills that contribute to a healthy and well-balanced lifestyle. As societal needs and dynamics change, the content of home economics programs continues to adapt to address emerging challenges and trends.
ABM
Account-Based Marketing (ABM): In the business and marketing context, ABM stands for Account-Based Marketing. It's a strategic approach where marketing efforts are focused on a specific set of high-value target accounts, rather than a broader audience. This approach involves personalized marketing campaigns tailored to the needs and characteristics of individual accounts, aiming to build stronger relationships and drive business growth.
